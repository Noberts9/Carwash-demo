Detailing Services Buffing

Buffing is the process where you use a machine and a cutting compound to remove light scratches and oxidation layer from the surface of your car. It also gives the car a brighter and glossy feel

Car Body Waxing

This is where we apply wax on the car body so as to provide a protective layer to the surface of the car and also make it shine. Waxing helps to prevent water spots from the surface of the car and minor scratches.

Rim Cleaning

This is where we clean the rims and chromes using special detergents so as to remove any corrosion stains and prevent accumulation of dirt that causes the corrosion.

Dashboard Polish

Helps to prevent cracks and fading of the rubber or plastic on the dashboard surface and also giving it a shiny and fresh look.

Upholstery Shampoo

This is where we clean the fabrics in the car (sit, roof and floor) using a spray extractor so as to remove dirt and stains. Upholstery helps to give your car interior a fresh look.

Interior Vacuum

Helps to remove dust particles from the car which if left to accumulate causes permanent dirt which is very involving and expensive to clean.

Engine Clean

Engine cleaning helps to remove accumulated dust or stains on your engine which can cause strain on how the engine works. It also helps to notice oil leaks and improving the general look of the engine.

Headlight Restoration

This is where we remove the oxidation layer from the surface of the headlight which makes the headlights look cloudy. Headlight restoration gives the headlight a clear look improving its aesthetic appeal and ability to project light properly giving the driver a proper vision.
